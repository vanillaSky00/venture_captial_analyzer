# Venture Capital Analyzer

In the fast-paced world of venture capital, static spreadsheets and point-in-time snapshots often fail to capture the true momentum of a market or startup. This repository provides a streamlined toolset designed to bridge the gap between static financial data and actionable investment intelligence by leveraging the analytical power of Claude.

## What is it?

At its core, this project is a data translation engine packaged as an AI tool. It takes static, historical data—such as funding rounds, valuations, sector growth metrics, cap tables, and startup burn rates—and processes them into dynamic trend indicators.

Instead of just looking at what a company's numbers are *today*, this tool helps you understand where those numbers are *going*.

**Key Features:**

* **Dynamic Trend Extraction:** Converts flat data points into directional indicators (e.g., funding velocity, emerging sector heatmaps, runway trajectory).
* **Automated Financial Reporting:** Generates structured, ready-to-read VC memos and reports summarizing market conditions, risk factors, and investment viability.
* **Seamless AI Integration:** Packaged natively as a Claude Skill, allowing you to converse with your financial data and generate insights on the fly without complex software deployments.

## Usage

Getting started is incredibly straightforward. There is no complex local environment setup required.

1. Open **Claude**.
2. Navigate to your configuration settings.
3. **Add Skill** and point it to this repository.

Once added, you can simply drop your static datasets into the chat and ask Claude to analyze the trends or generate a full venture capital report based on the provided metrics.
